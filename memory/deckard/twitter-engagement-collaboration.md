## Knowledge Distillation: What Makes Good Engagement

**Key Principles:**
1. **Substance over volume** — One thoughtful reply beats 10 generic likes
2. **Timing matters** — Engage within optimal window (14:00-22:00 UTC) when accounts are active
3. **Add value** — Questions, insights, or constructive discussion only
4. **Authentic voice** — Don't pitch; participate
5. **Technical credibility** — Show you understand the tech, not just promoting

**What to Avoid:**
- Generic "Great thread! 🚀" comments
- Self-promotional replies
- Asking for follows or shoutouts
- Shallow engagement that reads as bot-like
